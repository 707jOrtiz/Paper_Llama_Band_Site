Paper Llama

Paper Llama is an American progressive rock band from California originally formed in 2017 under the name Just For Kicks(JFK) by Jake Druzgala and Jacob Ortiz. Though a number of lineup changes followed, in 2020, Matt Baker and "The Mysterioso Raven" joined the group and the band name was changed to Paper Llama. Yeah, that's right, covid couldn't stop these bois. In fact, Paper Llama happens to be the greatest band ever. Other bands wish they could be us. We are seriously that good. You can check out all of our stuff on Youtube, Spotify and other places once we are done recording. For now you gotta wait. And to quote something my dad always told me, "Be patient or you will be a patient... in the hospital". It will be worth the wait. Oh also we just got a new guitar player. Thats pretty cool. Now we are better than the greatest band ever.
